# Brand Identity
Al-Gyas Exports: Premium Agricultural Products Worldwide

## 6. Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Vibe: Premium, Agricultural, Sustainability, Modern, Dynamic 3D feel
- Palette: 
  - Primary: Deep Earthy Green (#1B4332) for branding and trust
  - Secondary: Rich Gold (#D4AF37) for premium quality and accents
  - Background: Clean White (#FFFFFF) and Subtle Off-White (#F8F9FA)
  - Dark Mode: Very Dark Slate (#0F172A) with Green/Gold glows
- Typography: Inter/Roboto (Modern Sans-serif)
- Styles: Soft rounded corners (8px), subtle glassmorphism on floating cards, dynamic 3D-like hover effects with smooth shadows.
